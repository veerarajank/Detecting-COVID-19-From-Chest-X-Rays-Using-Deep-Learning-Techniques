About COVID-19

COVID-19, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, primarily affects the respiratory system, including the lungs. It can lead to symptoms like cough, shortness of breath, and pneumonia. In severe cases, it can cause acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS), which is a life-threatening condition where the lungs become severely inflamed and filled with fluid. The virus can also have long-term effects on the lungs, leading to conditions like post-COVID fibrosis. Protecting yourself with vaccination, wearing masks, and practicing good hygiene can reduce the risk of infection and lung-related complications.
